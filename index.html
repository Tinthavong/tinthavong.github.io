Fantasy Park

Fantasy Park is a first person shooter about a fantastical realm filled with magic and monsters... And guns.

Developed in Unity, this game idea came about from imagining "What would happen if you were sent into a fantasy world and all you had was a gun?" Fantasy Park answers that question. The player is thrown into a hostile area filled with dragons, ogres, zombies and more! The player can either take their time to enjoy Fantasy Park's sandbox world or they can fight their way out.

Causation

Gritty 2D action side scroller set in a cyberpunk world. A collaborative effort for Davenport University.

Causation was developed in Unity. Causation tells the story of a family of bounty hunters whose actions have a domino effect on not just their own lives, but the world around them. The player controls three characters and each of their stories revolve around hunting a bounty that eludes each of them. This escaped bounty has a profound effect on the world around them. I contributed by handling version control and merges on github, developing the core gameplay loop and varied weapons system based on the artists' and designer's plans. I also handled implementing character animations and handled creating cutscenes with Unity's timeline sequencer.

Faceplant

Physics based 3D platforming game focused on parkouring and latching onto skyscrapers. A collaborative thesis project for Davenport University.

Faceplant was developed in Unity. Faceplant is an arcadey 3D platformer that showcases slick maneuvering off of skyscrapers. The player uses the momentum from running, coupled with a grapple hook to swing around the environments. I contributed by creating an NPC AI that has simple behavior for the player to latch onto. I utilizied Unity's Shadergraph to implement shaders and visuals for the game. I created and implemented a highscore database for the game. I also created all the 3D models and assets.
